How to make Burger

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds meat
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Â¾ cup dry bread crumbs
  • 3 tablespoons evaporated milk
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • pepper,garlic

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill for high heat.
  2. Mix the Meat, egg, bread crumbs, evaporated milk, Worcestershire sauce, pepper, and garlic in a large bowl using your hands.
  3. Form the mixture into 8 burger patties.
  4. Lightly oil the grill grate. Grill patties until browned and no longer pink, about 5 minutes per side.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy
