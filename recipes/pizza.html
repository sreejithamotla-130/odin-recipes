How to make pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 French baguette
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • shredded mozzarella cheese
  • veggies
  • pepper,garlic

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Split baguette in half lengthwise. Warm pizza sauce in a microwave-safe bowl until hot, about 45 seconds.
  3. Spread pizza sauce on baguette, sprinkle on cheese, and scatter veggies
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is golden, about 18 minutes.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy
